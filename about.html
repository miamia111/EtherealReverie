About Si Wu

Si Wu is an independent artist based in Xiamen, China. Her practice moves between painting and mixed media, exploring the shifting boundary between reality and imagination.

Drawing inspiration from science fiction, mythology and contemporary visual culture, her work constructs dreamlike spaces where femininity, fear and the unknown coexist. Through luminous colour, symbolic figures and layered materials, she creates visual narratives that oscillate between the organic and the artificial, the sacred and the speculative.

Before focusing on her artistic practice, Si Wu studied Economics at the University of Bristol and later worked in China’s animation and gaming industries, including roles as a manga editor, anime convention curator and product manager for 3D avatar tools. These experiences shaped her interest in speculative worlds, digital aesthetics and narrative imagery.

She is currently based in Xiamen and will soon continue her practice in the United Kingdom.
