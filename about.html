About Si Wu

Si Wu is an independent artist currently based in Beijing. Working across painting, installation and conceptual practice, her work explores how memory, time and space intersect and shift within personal experience.

She initially studied oil painting at the China Academy of Art before moving towards mixed media and spatial practice. Everyday objects, found photographs and traces of the body often become starting points. Through repetition, layering and deliberate gaps, private memories are transformed into a visual language that can be rewritten by the viewer.

In recent years she has participated in group exhibitions and residency programmes in China and abroad, with works held in both private and institutional collections. She sees art as an “unfinished conversation” — each work is only completed in the gaze of its audience.
