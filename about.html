About Si Wu

I am an independent artist currently based in Xiamen, China. I work across painting and mixed media, exploring the shifting boundary between reality and imagination.

My practice draws on science fiction, mythology, and contemporary visual culture to construct spaces where femininity, fear, and beauty coexist.

I aim to explore how the language of cosmic horror and religious symbolism can be reinterpreted to create a contemporary feminist art practice.I am interested in transforming traditional sacred symbols into a new maternal narrative,one that converts submission and silence into empowerment and presence.

Before focusing on my artistic practice, I studied Economics at the University of Bristol and later worked in China’s animation and gaming industries—as a manga editor, anime convention curator, and product manager for 3D avatar tools. These experiences continue to shape my interest in constructed worlds, digital aesthetics, and narrative structures.
